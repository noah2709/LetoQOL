# LetoQOL

**LetoQOL** bundles practical **quality-of-life** tweaks for *World of Warcraft* in one lightweight addon. Every feature can be toggled from a single options window—no scattered slash commands or separate addons required.

---

## Highlights

- **Modular** — turn off anything you don’t use; settings persist per character via `LetoQOLDB`.
- **In-game UI** — type **`/qol`** to configure all modules.
- **Localized** — English (**enUS**) and German (**deDE**) client strings included.

---

## Features

### Character Pane Enhancement

Richer **Character** (**C**) window without replacing Blizzard’s UI:

- Item level on gear, optional **average item level** (with decimals)
- **Enchant** and **gem** display, plus **highlight for missing enchants**
- **Durability** per slot and **total durability** summary
- Item level coloring by **item quality** or a **custom** color

---

### Gateway Tracker

On-screen reminder when your **Engineering wormhole / gateway item** is off cooldown and ready to use.

- Optional **combat-only** mode so the hint only appears while fighting
- Custom **text size**, **color**, and **position** (unlock, drag, lock)
- **Test / preview** to line up the frame

---

### Pet Reminder

For **Hunter**, **Death Knight**, and **Warlock** characters:

- Warns if your pet is **missing** or stuck in **passive** stance
- Adjustable **text size**, **color**, and **position**
- Optional **show only in party or raid**

---

### Focus Interrupt

Dungeon coordination helpers:

- On **ready check**, posts your chosen **raid target icon** for your focus interrupt in **group chat** when you’re in a party or raid
- **Icon picker** (star through skull)
- **Mark focus** or **mouseover** mode via a secure button — bind under **ESC → Key Bindings → AddOns → LetoQOL**

---

### Auto Role Accept

Automatically **accepts your role** when the group leader queues for matchmade content, so one less click in busy sessions.

---

### NPC Helper

Merchant convenience:

- **Auto repair** (optional **guild bank** repair when available)
- **Auto sell junk**

Chat messages confirm repairs and junk sales when applicable.

---

### Teleport Compendium

**World Map** integration for **Hero’s Path** (and related) teleports:

- **Button on the map** opens a scrollable list grouped by expansion
- **Click-to-cast** secure rows; names are derived from spell descriptions where possible (with **English** and **German** phrasing support)
- **Duplicate** flyout entries are collapsed; layout avoids clipping long dungeon names

---

### Hide Talking Head

Suppresses the **Talking Head** frame during quests and events when you want fewer popups on screen.
